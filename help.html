♠ PokerFace Help

Everything you need to know to run a smooth estimation session.

Quick Start

  1. Create a session — click "Create Session" on the home page.
  2. Enter your name and pick your role (Developer, QA, DevOps/SRE, Designer, or Business).
  3. Share the link — click "Copy Link" and send it to your team.
  4. Pick your estimate — tap a Fibonacci card (0, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21, ?, or ☕).
  5. Reveal votes — once everyone has voted, click "Show Votes".
  6. Discuss & repeat — click "New Round" when ready for the next story.

Roles & Grouped Estimation

When joining, you choose a role. This affects how results are displayed:

  • Participants are grouped by role in the sidebar — each role gets its own card showing the member count.
  • After reveal, you see per-role stats: each group's average and consensus (Agreement / Close / Discuss).
  • Total Estimate is the sum of each role's average. For example, if Developers average 5 and QA averages 3, the total is 8 points — representing the combined effort for the ticket.

Timer

Keep estimation rounds focused with a shared countdown timer.

  • Pick a duration from the dropdown (30s, 1:00, 1:30, or 2:00) and click Start.
  • The timer is synced for all participants — everyone sees the same countdown.
  • The progress bar turns yellow at 50% remaining and pulses red at 20%.
  • When time runs out, it shows "Time's up!" and auto-hides after 3 seconds.
  • The timer resets automatically when you start a new round.

Spectator Mode

Perfect for PMs, stakeholders, or anyone who wants to observe without influencing estimates.

  • Check "Join as spectator" on the name entry page.
  • Spectators can see all votes and results but cannot vote.
  • Spectators appear in a separate "Spectators" panel with a 👁 badge.
  • Spectator votes are excluded from all statistics.
  • Your spectator preference is remembered for next time.

Emoji Reactions

React to the results after votes are revealed.

  • A floating reaction bar appears in the bottom-right corner after reveal.
  • Available reactions: 👍 🤔 😱 🎯 💀
  • Reactions are visible to all participants — emojis float up the screen in real-time.
  • Reactions reset when a new round starts.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Vote and control rounds without touching the mouse.

  • 0 – 9 — Vote by card index (0=first card, 9=last card)
  • R — Reveal votes (same as "Show Votes" button)
  • N — New round (same as "New Round" button)

Shortcuts are disabled when typing in input fields or when in spectator mode.

Confetti Celebration

When votes are revealed and every role group is in full agreement, confetti rains down to celebrate the consensus. No action needed — it just happens when the team aligns.

Voting Scale

PokerFace uses a modified Fibonacci sequence:

  • 0, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21 — Standard Fibonacci story points
  • ? — "I don't have enough information to estimate"
  • ☕ — "I need a break" or "this is not estimable"

Non-numeric votes (? and ☕) are excluded from average calculations.

Consensus Indicators

After reveal, each role group shows a consensus status:

  • ✅ Agreement — Everyone voted the same value.
  • ≈ Close — Votes are within 3 points of each other.
  • ⚠ Discuss — Votes are spread apart — time to talk it out.
